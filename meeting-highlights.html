1. Strategy & Pricing Framework

Margin Strategy

  • Maintain "Sweet Spot" between Maggie’s ₹9 price point and premium positioning.
  • Avoid historical mistake: Do not launch with high base margins (difficult to revise down).
  • Standardize market commission at 15% markup (Standard is 9-10%).
  • Ensure gram-to-gram price parity with Maggie for mass penetration.

Distribution Economics

  • Distributor margin analysis required vs top market competition.
  • Wholesale margin calculations needed for bulk channel viability.
  • Retailer margin considerations must prioritize pilot store buy-in.

2. Market Entry & Channel Strategy

Launch Framework

  • 100-store pilot across 4 cities (Specific focus on Delhi & Gurgaon).
  • Phased rollout using the Three-tier launch plan.
  • Balance Modern Trade visibility vs Traditional Trade volume.
  • Positioning: Decide between category development or established category.

Promotional Schemes

  • Volume-based schemes for Small, Medium, and Large retailers.
  • On-ground promoter support depending on specific trade flow.
  • Differentiate National vs Regional scheme structures.
  • Active sampling activities during the first 30 days of launch.

3. Operational & Distribution Logistics

Key Logistics & Demand Challenges

  • Billing: Enforce minimum billing quantity restrictions.
  • Order Velocity: No "breathing space" between orders during initial phase.
  • Growth: High logistics demand management for startup operations.
  • Stock: Dedicated stock management for new brand SKU tracking.
