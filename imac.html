1-Store identification & Store visit (CCR)
Store Identification:
  • Search for new potential stores in the assigned area.
  • Verify store details and business potential.
  • Collect complete store's information as per policy (Geo Tag Location, GST no, Mobile, Address).
  • Share details to system team to add in software.
Store Visit (CCR- Call Compliance Report):
  • Visit stores as per assigned route plan.
  • Check stock availability and discuss product requirements.
  • Resolve customer concerns and handle objections.
  • Update visit status in the system as per CCR norms.
2-Order Booking (BCP) & Order transfer to Billing
Order Booking (BCP-Basic Call Procedure):
  • Follow the BCP Step for order taking.
  • Verify stock availability.
  • Enter orders correctly in the Bizom application only.
  • Confirm order details and delivery date before submission.
Order Transfer to Billing:
  • Verify booked orders.
  • Transfer orders to billing on time to branch server via application.
  • Coordinate with billing and logistics teams if changes required.
3-Daily review & Monthly Review
Daily Review:
  • Review daily sales and visit target performance.
  • Check all the stores CCR and pending activities.
  • Discuss challenges, identify issues and action plans.
  • Next Day PFD target plan.
  • Fill the review book/Tracker summary as per company policy.
Monthly Review:
  • Monthly review scorecard format will set as per tracking mechanism.
  • Measure overall focus area and analyze monthly sales performance.
  • Review target achievement and market opportunities.
